Our Story

Café Barcelona was born out of a passion for great coffee and even better community. Nestled in the heart of the city, our café is a local favorite for its inviting atmosphere, friendly faces, and lovingly prepared drinks.

Whether you’re a digital nomad looking for your next remote office, a tourist seeking authentic local flavor, or a neighborhood regular, we’re here to make you feel at home.

What We Value

  • Quality: We source only the finest beans and ingredients.
  • Community: We support local artists, makers, and causes.
  • Sustainability: Our practices aim to reduce waste and promote eco-conscious choices.

Come Visit Us!

Located just minutes from Plaça Catalunya, we’re open 7 days a week with indoor and outdoor seating. Stop by, sip slow, and stay awhile.
